Who Are We?

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation. Debuting on July 22, 2022, the group quickly gained popularity for their fresh and nostalgic approach to K-pop, blending Y2K aesthetics with modern music trends. Known for their effortless charm and natural image, NewJeans stands out with a unique sound that incorporates R&B, pop, and alternative influences. Their self-titled debut EP, featuring hits like Attention and Hype Boy, propelled them to stardom, earning them critical acclaim and a strong international fanbase. With their distinctive concept and innovative music, NewJeans continues to redefine the K-pop landscape.

Check It Out!

NewJeans collaborated with Coca-Cola as global ambassadors, blending their fresh and youthful image with the brand's identity.



As part of the partnership, they released the song "Zero" in April 2023, a promotional track for Coca-Cola Zero Sugar, featuring catchy lyrics referencing the beverage. The collaboration extended to a remix with rapper J.I.D and participation in the Coca-Cola campaign "Be Who You Are (Real Magic)," alongside Jon Batiste, Camilo, and J.I.D. Through this partnership, NewJeans engaged in exclusive content, live performances, and promotional activities, reinforcing their growing global influence in both music and branding.
